About

Spread Love

I truly believe that whatever you do in life, you have to do it with LOVE. This is the main reason why I started Nat Larcipretti Events!

I have both the necessary education (Bachelor in Hotel & Events Management) and expertise to help you put together an unforgettable wedding/event. Whether you have always dreamed about it or are completely overwhelmed with the entire planning idea… trust me, we will have fun together!

My goal is to fill your event with personality, make sure it is even more than you and your guests have always dreamed about. Classic, Rustic, Fairy Tale, Vintage, Gatsby, Sports, Carnival, Circus, Superhero, Disney – whatever your style is, we can make it happen! You are very welcome here! Spreading love is my way to change the world!

Services


Planning your own wedding is definitely a full time job! We know that not everyone is lucky enough to be able to dedicate hours to bring to life all those ideas you’ve been saving on Pinterest and Instagram for a long time. That is why we are here! Let us help you enjoy a stress-free planning and live your dream from the “I’m Engaged” moment until you say “I do” (Well, we will still hang around for a bit after that!).

We believe that the only thing YOU HAVE TO when planning your wedding is: do what YOU want! Make sure everything has your personal touch and it will be such a memorable day for you, your friends and your family!

Whether you need a full planning option or only “a hand” on your big day, we will customize a package that fits your needs - and your budget.

We also organize all sorts of social events: engagement parties, bachelor/ette parties, birthdays, milestone ceremonies, or… need help with a creative proposal?

Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Learn about your story, dreams and expectations
  • Budget Management
  • Creation of a custom website with your story, RSVP, Gift Registry and pictures
  • Digital Save the Date Design
  • Vendors Recommendation + Pricing Negotiation + Management on the day of your event
  • Site Visit & Inspection
  • Review contracts + manage last payments
  • Weekly Report updates
  • Seating Chart with Floor plans development
  • Rehearsal Dinner
  • Wedding Day Itinerary
  • Execution of your event from start to finish
  • Need help planning your honeymoon? We can do that too!
